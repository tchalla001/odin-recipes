Recipes

some tasty ugali with amtumbo

ingredients

  • 2kg meat
  • 2kg flour
  • tomato's
  • pepper
  • onion
  • cooking fat

steps

  1. Cut the onions into small equal pieces and put them on a clean plate
  2. Add the cooking fat on a sufuria then add the onions,when is brown add the matumbo and mix them together....season and let them cook
  3. As they cook,boil 3 litres of water for cooking ugali,when it boils add 1 table spoon of blue band, add the flour in stir until cooked
  4. For the kachumbari,cut the onions and tomato's on a clean chopping board,mix them together,and serve your meal
